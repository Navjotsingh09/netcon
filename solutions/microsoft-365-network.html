Here's What a Thorough Office 365 Readiness Assessment Involves

Network design, connectivity, bandwidth, latency, endpoint performance, and security controls are examined during an Office 365 Readiness Assessment.

Consultants assess traffic flows, routing, firewalls, configurations, and authentication methods. User behaviour and application usage are reviewed to identify issues before deployment causes disruption.

The evaluation provides reports with risk analysis, optimisation recommendations, and actionable steps for reliable Microsoft 365 operations.
