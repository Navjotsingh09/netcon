Frequently Asked Questions

01What is a cyber security review?
A cybersecurity review assesses your organisation's networks, procedures, and security controls to identify weaknesses, evaluate risk exposure, and provide improvement recommendations.
02What is included in a cyber risk assessment?
A cyber risk assessment covers network architecture, access controls, firewall configurations, patch management, authentication practices, and user security awareness, resulting in a prioritised risk register.
03How often should a cyber security review be conducted?
We recommend annual reviews as a minimum, with additional reviews following significant infrastructure changes, new deployments, or any security incidents.
