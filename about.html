The Origin of Network Consultancy

Since 1995, Network Consultancy has operated as a specialised IT and network engineering brand. We built this company to bring mid-sized businesses out of basic infrastructure and cybersecurity limitations to enterprise-level technical expertise at a price that doesn't restrict their growth.

From the public to the private sectors across the UK, our deep expertise in networks, security, and infrastructure supports operations to eliminate downtime and vulnerabilities that disrupt daily business.

How We Operate

Our company operates with a business-focused mindset to give clients a partner-like experience rather than ticketing queues.

We approach your network with:

1) Consultancy that is backed by Cisco-certified engineers, assessing network, design, and vulnerability audits.

2) 24/7 Senior-engineer support access, where you can reach us exactly when network emergencies hit.

3) Managed engineering where the accountability of your system uptime rests entirely on our team.

Our Engineers' Proficiency

We believe strong engineering is necessary for achieving stronger results. Our team of Cisco-certified engineers has practical designing and deployment experience within the UK's top companies.

We apply this enterprise-level discipline to your daily infrastructure, resolving complex routing issues, managing hardware upgrades, and securing your data perimeters.
