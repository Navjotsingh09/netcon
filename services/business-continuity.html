Operational Disruptions Cost UK Businesses £10,000 Average.

The Cyber Security Breaches Survey from GOV.UK shows that one network incident costs UK businesses an average of £10,000. The number seems staggering because work stops. Staff cannot access their files, and customers cannot buy or get support. The reality is that most office networks rely on a single internet line or a single server room.

If a local power cut or supplier outage hits that single point, the whole company goes offline. Our business continuity consultancy finds these weak points in your current setup. Through operational resilience consulting, we install automatic backup links and secondary power systems. If your primary line fails, the network switches to the backup line instantly. Results: your business stays online, and you avoid the cost of an outage.
