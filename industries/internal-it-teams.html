Frequently Asked Questions

01What exactly is network consultancy, and how does it assist the internal IT team?
Network consultants provide specialised networking knowledge that the internal IT team's schedule does not permit them to develop within the organisation.

The consultant will design, troubleshoot, and secure networks in conjunction with the internal IT team. The consultant applies their expertise to the networks that require more focus than the internal IT team can provide in their daily activities.
02When should an organisation use network consultancy services?
Organisations usually bring in consultancy when internal teams are stretched, network changes are high risk, or performance issues continue despite repeated fixes.

It is especially useful during growth, office moves, mergers, or infrastructure refresh projects where specialist design and migration planning are needed.
03What are the benefits of working with a network consultancy?
You gain access to specialist expertise, improved network stability, stronger security posture, and faster delivery of complex networking projects.

Your internal team keeps ownership while benefiting from additional capability where it matters most.
