Frequently Asked Questions

01What network security measures do financial institutions use?
To safeguard sensitive financial data and ensure regulatory compliance, financial institutions use layered security measures, including firewalls, encryption, intrusion detection, ongoing monitoring, and stringent access controls.
02What are the most common cyber threats financial organisations face?
Phishing, ransomware, account takeover, and distributed denial-of-service attacks are among the most common threats. Financial institutions reduce risk by hardening access, monitoring traffic, and strengthening response controls.
03Why is continuous network monitoring important?
Continuous monitoring helps detect unusual behaviour early so teams can respond before issues become outages or security incidents.
