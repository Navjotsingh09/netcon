Frequently Asked Questions

01What network security measures do financial institutions use?
They usually combine layered security controls, access management, monitoring, encryption, and response procedures so sensitive financial data stays protected and systems remain available.
02What are the most common cyber threats for financial organisations?
Phishing, ransomware, account takeover, insider misuse, and denial-of-service attacks are all common risks. We design networks to reduce the impact of each one.
03Why is continuous monitoring important?
Continuous monitoring helps identify unusual behaviour early, so teams can respond before a small issue becomes an outage or a security incident.
