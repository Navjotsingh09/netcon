Harry Dobbs Design - Network & Security Infrastructure Support

Harry Dobbs Design operates in a creative environment where reliable connectivity and secure systems are critical to daily operations.

This case study highlights how Network Consultancy addressed its connectivity and security challenges.

Overview

Harry Dobbs Design is an award-winning London-based architecture, urban design, and product design practice, delivering innovative projects for clients across the UK and internationally.

The business relies on secure, high-performance IT systems to support collaboration, project delivery, and the protection of valuable design data.

Network Consultancy partnered with Harry Dobbs Design to modernise and support its network and security infrastructure, ensuring employees have reliable connectivity, enhanced security, and a resilient platform for future growth.

Challenge

As the business evolved, elements of the existing network and security infrastructure had reached the end of their useful lifecycle.

Connectivity performance, wireless coverage, data protection, and system resilience required improvement to support the demands of a modern design practice.

The organisation needed a secure and reliable infrastructure that would protect business-critical data, improve user experience, and provide a foundation capable of supporting future business requirements without ongoing infrastructure concerns.

Solution

Network Consultancy designed and implemented a comprehensive infrastructure refresh focused on security, resilience, and performance.

A high-availability firewall solution was introduced to strengthen perimeter security and remove single points of failure. The wireless network was upgraded with centrally managed access points, providing consistent coverage and improved connectivity throughout the office environment.

We also modernised the organisation's storage and backup systems, ensuring critical project data is securely protected and recoverable in the event of an outage or system failure.

By consolidating management and monitoring capabilities, the overall infrastructure became easier to administer and maintain.

Result

  • Enhanced security through a resilient firewall platform.
  • Improved protection of business-critical project and client data.
  • Consistent and reliable wireless connectivity across the workplace.
  • Reduced operational risk through the replacement of ageing infrastructure.
  • Improved business continuity and data recovery capabilities.
  • A scalable and dependable IT platform that supports ongoing business growth.
  • Simplified infrastructure management and monitoring.
