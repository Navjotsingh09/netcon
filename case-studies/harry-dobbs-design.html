Project Overview

Harry Dobbs Design

Harry Dobbs Design is a creative agency requiring reliable, secure network infrastructure to support their design operations and growing team. They needed to modernise their network, improve wireless coverage, and strengthen their security posture.

Services Delivered

  • Firewall and security improvements
  • Managed wireless LAN
  • Network upgrade
  • Backup and storage

The Challenge

The agency’s existing network infrastructure was aging and unable to reliably support their growing team. Wireless coverage was inconsistent, security controls were outdated, and the network was not designed to handle the bandwidth demands of modern creative workloads.

The Solution

Network Consultancy delivered a comprehensive network modernisation programme including firewall and security improvements, a new managed wireless LAN, network upgrade, and backup and storage enhancements — providing a modern, reliable, and secure infrastructure.

Results

The improvements resulted in reliable, consistent wireless coverage throughout the office, significantly strengthened security, and a network infrastructure capable of supporting the agency’s creative workloads and future growth.
