Antal International - Global SASE Transformation

Antal International needed secure, consistent access to business systems across its locations.

This case study highlights how Network Consultancy delivered a global SASE transformation.

Overview

Antal International is a global recruitment and executive search organisation with offices and users operating across multiple countries.

As the business continued to grow internationally, secure and consistent access to business applications became essential for supporting a distributed workforce.

Network Consultancy partnered with Antal International to modernise its network security architecture through a global Secure Access Service Edge (SASE) transformation, delivering a scalable, cloud-first platform that supports secure connectivity from any location.

Challenge

Managing traditional security infrastructure across multiple international offices was becoming increasingly complex.

Security appliances at individual locations required separate administration, creating additional operational overhead and making it difficult to maintain consistent security policies across the organisation.

As the global footprint expanded, the business needed a more agile approach that could provide secure access to corporate resources regardless of user location, while simplifying management and supporting future growth.

Solution

Network Consultancy designed and delivered a cloud-based SASE architecture that replaced traditional location-dependent security infrastructure with a centrally managed security and networking platform.

By moving security controls to the cloud, policies could be applied consistently across all users and locations from a single management framework.

The new architecture enabled secure access to business applications for both office-based and remote employees, without relying on traditional network backhauling or site-specific infrastructure.

The solution also simplified the deployment of new locations, allowing offices and users to be connected quickly and securely without the need for dedicated on site security hardware.

Result

  • Consistent and secure access to business applications across all global locations.
  • Centralised security policy management throughout the organisation.
  • Improved user experience for both office-based and remote workers.
  • Reduced reliance on traditional on-premises security infrastructure.
  • Faster onboarding of new locations and users.
  • Increased scalability to support international growth.
  • Enhanced flexibility through a cloud-delivered security framework.
  • Simplified operational management and reduced administrative overhead.
