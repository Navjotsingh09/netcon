Project Overview

Senate Computers

Senate Computers, a technology reseller, required reliable managed network support and ongoing infrastructure improvements to maintain the performance and security of their operations.

Services Delivered

  • Managed network support
  • Infrastructure improvements
  • Security enhancements

The Challenge

Senate Computers needed a trusted network partner to provide responsive managed support while delivering targeted infrastructure improvements to address performance and security issues.

The Solution

Network Consultancy provided ongoing managed network support with a programme of planned infrastructure improvements, ensuring Senate Computers benefited from both reactive issue resolution and proactive infrastructure development.

Results

The partnership delivered improved network reliability, enhanced security controls, and a more proactive approach to infrastructure management.
