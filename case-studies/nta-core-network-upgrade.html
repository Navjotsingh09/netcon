NTA - Core Network Support & Managed Services Partnership

This case study highlights how Network Consultancy helped NTA, a VoIP and internet service provider, replace core network equipment across three data centres.

We built a scalable foundation for NTA that's now supporting a growing customer base and future demand.

Overview

NTA is a VoIP and internet service provider delivering reliable communication and connectivity services to a growing customer base.

As NTA's trusted network partner, Network Consultancy provides ongoing support and maintenance services for the organisation's core network infrastructure under a dedicated support agreement.

Through this partnership, we help ensure the network remains resilient, scalable, and capable of meeting increasing customer demand while supporting the continued evolution of NTA's services.

Challenge

As NTA's customer base expanded, the core network infrastructure supporting critical ISP and VoIP services required ongoing optimisation, proactive management, and expert technical support to maintain performance and reliability.

The business needed a trusted partner capable of providing specialist networking expertise, helping to reduce operational risk, maintain service continuity, and ensure the network could support future growth and emerging technologies without impacting customers.

Solution

Network Consultancy works closely with NTA as an ongoing support partner, providing specialist assistance across the organisation's core network environment.

Our services include proactive network support, infrastructure optimisation, technical consultancy, performance monitoring, and guidance on future network enhancements.

By maintaining and supporting the core infrastructure across NTA's data centre environments, we help ensure the platform remains stable, secure, and scalable.

Through our continued engagement, NTA benefits from access to experienced network engineers who assist with maintaining service availability, resolving technical issues, and supporting the long-term development of the network.

Result

  • Reliable and stable core network operations.
  • Ongoing expert support for critical ISP and VoIP services.
  • Improved network resilience and operational confidence.
  • A scalable infrastructure capable of supporting business growth.
  • Reduced operational risk through proactive management and specialist expertise.
  • A long term technology partnership that supports NTA's evolving business requirements.
