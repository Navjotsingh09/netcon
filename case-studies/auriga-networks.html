Project Overview

Auriga Networks

Auriga Networks required a trusted network partner to provide ongoing managed support alongside a programme of network upgrades and infrastructure optimisation to improve their operational efficiency and platform reliability.

Services Delivered

  • Managed network support
  • Network upgrades
  • Infrastructure optimisation

The Challenge

Auriga Networks needed to improve the performance and reliability of their existing infrastructure while maintaining operational continuity. They required a partner who could deliver both strategic improvement and responsive day-to-day support.

The Solution

Network Consultancy provided an integrated programme of managed support, targeted network upgrades, and infrastructure optimisation. Our team worked closely with Auriga to deliver improvements in a phased approach minimising operational disruption.

Results

The programme delivered measurable improvements in infrastructure performance and reliability. Auriga Networks now benefits from a more efficient, better-managed network environment with ongoing support from the Network Consultancy team.
