Auriga Networks - Managed Network Support & Infrastructure Development

Auriga Networks delivers broadcasting and satellite services that require uninterrupted infrastructure.

Support continued alongside ongoing network changes, keeping pace as business and technology requirements shifted over time.

Overview

Auriga Networks operates within the broadcasting and satellite communications sector, where network availability, performance, and reliability are essential to daily operations.

Network Consultancy has provided ongoing network support and infrastructure consultancy services, helping Auriga Networks maintain business-critical services while continuously evolving its network environment to meet changing operational and technology requirements.

Challenge

Broadcasting and satellite services depend on highly available infrastructure, leaving little tolerance for network disruption or performance degradation.

As business requirements evolved and technology demands increased, the network needed to adapt without affecting live services.

The challenge was to deliver continuous improvements, maintain operational stability, and minimise risk while supporting a complex and constantly changing infrastructure environment.

Solution

Network Consultancy delivered a proactive support and infrastructure improvement programme designed to maintain service continuity whilst enabling ongoing network evolution.

Rather than implementing large-scale disruptive changes, enhancements were introduced through a phased and carefully managed approach.

This allowed infrastructure upgrades, performance improvements, and network optimisation activities to be carried out while maintaining the availability of critical services.

Through ongoing monitoring, technical support, and regular infrastructure reviews, potential issues were identified and addressed before impacting business operations.

This ensured the network remained aligned with both current operational requirements and future technology developments.

Result

  • Continuous availability of business-critical broadcasting and satellite services.
  • Improved network stability and operational resilience.
  • Reduced risk associated with infrastructure changes and upgrades.
  • Successful delivery of enhancements without disruption to live services.
  • Increased capability to support evolving business and technology requirements.
  • Consistent network performance as operational demands increased.
  • A scalable infrastructure foundation supporting long-term growth.
